Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?
Both yearly and monthly plans include a 7-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends. Cancel anytime during the trial at no cost through Settings ‚Üí Subscriptions.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Open PureFast ‚Üí go to your Profile tab ‚Üí tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.
Is my data private?
Yes. All your fasting data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or sell personal health information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Does PureFast work with Apple Health?
Yes. PureFast can read and write fasting, weight, and hydration data to Apple Health. You can enable or disable this in the app's Settings.
Is PureFast medical advice?
No. PureFast is a wellness tracking tool, not a medical device. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting any fasting program. See our in-app medical disclaimer and Sources & Citations for referenced research.
