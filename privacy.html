Privacy Policy for PureFast

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Introduction

PureFast ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Health Data

With your explicit permission, PureFast may access and store:

  • Fasting session data (start times, end times, duration)
  • Health metrics from Apple Health (with your consent)
  • Weight and body measurements you choose to log

2.2 Account Information

We collect basic profile information you provide:

  • Name (optional)
  • Age and gender (optional, for personalized insights)
  • Fasting goals and preferences

2.3 Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app:

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Crash reports and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and personalize your fasting experience
  • Track your progress and display statistics
  • Send you optional notifications about your fasting schedule
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

All your health data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your fasting data on external servers. Optional iCloud sync uses Apple's encrypted infrastructure.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Apple Health

If you grant permission, PureFast integrates with Apple Health to read and write health data. This data remains controlled by you through iOS Health settings.

5.2 In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not store your payment information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data within the app
  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete all your data permanently
  • Revoke HealthKit permissions through iOS Settings

7. Children's Privacy

PureFast is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page.
